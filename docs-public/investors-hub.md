# The Flight Club - Investors Hub

Welcome to the **TFC 2.0 Public Pages Forum Post**. This hub provides information for prospective and current investors in the Flight Club system.

## Overview

Flight Club is a collaborative initiative designed to help players earn, invest, and grow in a structured community. This document outlines the public-facing policies and opportunities for investment and contribution.

## Investment Strategy

- Contribute plushies, flowers, drugs, and other tradeable items.
- Receive monthly bonuses based on FIFO-based profit calculations.
- All accounting is transparent and managed via an open ledger.

## Bonuses

- 50% of net profit goes to contributing members
- Calculated automatically
- Paid out monthly

## Stock and Point Sales

- Stock Benefit Blocks: 10% retained by manager, 90% goes to club
- Points sold above market for profit
- Auto-pricing and margin logic built in

## Awards & Titles

- Participate in NFS (Need For Speed) competitions
- Earn multipliers for loyalty and contribution
- Top 10 earners get monthly cash bonuses

## Developer & Manager Pay

- Fixed retainer for active roles
- Extra pay for point sales, BB handling, and technical development

## Future Roadmap

- Expanding automation
- Enhanced transparency dashboards
- Additional reward systems and recognition tiers
