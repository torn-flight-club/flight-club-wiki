### Top Gun: Need For Speed 2.0

**4-week cycle:**  

* 1 week @ 20k points → **Merlin** = $2m  
* 2 weeks @ 20k points → **Jester** = $2.4m  
* 4 weeks @ 20k points → **Viper** = $3m

**Points:**

* 10k points for 48 hours flying/week
* NFS items give ~0.2 points per flight-minute to obtain
