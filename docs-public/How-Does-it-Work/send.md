title: How to Send Items?
sidebar_position: 4
---

1. Go to your inventory. 
2. Select the item that you will be contributing and click the send buttom. 
![send](https://file.garden/Zd005bwq23DtYCIi/Flight-Club/sends.png)
3. Select [MIA](https://www.torn.com/profiles.php?XID=131289) or [GalloInfligo](https://www.torn.com/profiles.php?XID=2133394), Type the amount of items you want to send and click Send.
4. Plushies and Flowers should be sent to only [MIA](https://www.torn.com/profiles.php?XID=131289).
5. Drugs and Other Items (Temps) should be only sent to [GalloInfligo](https://www.torn.com/profiles.php?XID=2133394).

> After sending your items please wait at least 10 minutes, before planning to withdraw your balance.
