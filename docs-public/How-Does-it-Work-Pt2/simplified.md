1. Fly and buy Plushies or Flowers, or selected Drugs and Other Items (Temps).
2. Send Flowers or Plushies to [MIA](https://www.torn.com/profiles.php?XID=131289).
3. Send Drugs and Other Items (Temps) to [GalloInfligo](https://www.torn.com/profiles.php?XID=2133394).
4. Wait a few minutes for it to register your contribution. You can see it popping up in Discord Contributions channel. 
5. Check the dashboard to check your Outstanding Balance for all items you have sent and the prizes you have earned.
6. Request payment when you need it by sending $1 to all managers, using the payment request button on the dashboard, or using /requestpay command in discord.
7. No managers need to be online for your request to be seen by our team.
8. Don't send anymore items until you have recieved your cash.
9. Rinse and Repeat.
